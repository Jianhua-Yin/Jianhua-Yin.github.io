Posted on 11-26-2023 In Tools , 2DCOS

Two-dimensional Correlation Analysis facilitates the visualization of complex spectra with numerous overlapping peaks by spreading them across a second dimension.

Brief overview of 2D correlation spectroscopy

History of 2D COS

Initially developed approximately 30 years ago in NMR spectroscopy, two-dimensional spectroscopy facilitated the visualization of complex spectra characterized by numerous overlapping peaks by distributing them across a second dimension. While NMR remains the predominant technique in two-dimensional spectroscopy, this approach has been extended to other forms, proving particularly useful across various spectroscopic techniques.
