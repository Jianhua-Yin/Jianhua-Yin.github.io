Understanding of Powder Diffraction Patterns

Posted on 07-01-2020 Edited on 08-25-2023 In Pattern , Powder Diffraction Word count in article: 83 Reading time ≈ 1 mins.

The best way to understand how structure information is encoded in a powder diffraction pattern is to view it as a series of discrete diffraction peaks(Bragg reflections) superimposed over a consecutive background. Despite the background can provide infomation about the crystallinity of the specimen and a few other material properties, the primary focus in analyzing material structure remains on the Bragg reflection peaks.
